How to play :
- unzip "OperationWolf.zip"
- launch "OperationWolf.exe"

Description : 
You are a special forces agent and your mission is 
to save hostages from mercenaries' claws.
You lose when you don't have any remaining HP.
You win when all ennemies are dead and all hostages are rescued.

Controls :
- Aim with mouse
- Left click to shoot
- Right click to reload your weapon
- Escape to return on previous menu

Cheat Key :
- H to heal 3HP
- M to earn 1 magazine
- K to lose 3HP
